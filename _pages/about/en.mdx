### Gear and Workflow

I am a dual-boot user. For daily activities, especially for blogging, coding, and web development, I more often use **Linux**. The Linux environment gives me the freedom and control I need. However, I can't completely move away from **Windows 11**. There are times, especially when work demands intensive use of Microsoft Excel, that I switch to Windows.

My philosophy is simple: use the right tool for the right job.
