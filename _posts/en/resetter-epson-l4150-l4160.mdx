Does your Epson L4150 or L4160 printer suddenly stop working with a notification like **"Ink pad is at the end of its service life"** or **"Service required"**? Don't worry, this is a very common issue that you can fix yourself at home.

The cause is the **ink pad counter** — an internal counter that records how much ink has been absorbed by the waste ink pads. Once it reaches its maximum limit, the printer will automatically lock itself as a sign that the pads need to be replaced or reset. The solution is to use a tool called the **Epson Adjustment Program** (Resetter) to reset the counter so the printer can function normally again.
