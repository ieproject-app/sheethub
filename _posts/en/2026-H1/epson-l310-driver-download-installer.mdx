## Basic Printer Settings

For efficient use and to maintain the longevity of your printer, it is important to understand the basic settings.

1.  Open **Settings > Bluetooth & devices > Printers & scanners**.
2.  Find and select **EPSON L310 Series**, then click **Printing preferences**.
3.  In the **Main** tab, you can adjust:
    *   **Paper Size:** Usually A4.
    *   **Orientation:** Portrait or Landscape.
    *   **Color:** Color or Grayscale printing.
    *   **Quality:** Standard (faster) or High (slower, better results).

![Epson L310 printing preferences settings](/images/_posts/printer/epson/driver-epson-l310/epson-l310-14.webp)

In the **Maintenance** tab, you can perform maintenance tasks like "Nozzle Check" or "Head Cleaning" if the print quality is poor or showing streaks.
